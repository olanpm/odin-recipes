Falafel Wrap Recipe

Description

Delicious recipe for a plant-based Falafel Wrap.

Level up your lunch game with this delicious wholesome Falafel wrap. Bursting with flavors from the crispy falafel, fresh veggies, creamy hummus and tahini dressing that makes you want more with every bite!

Ingredients

  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Pasta of Choice
  • Vegetable Stock
  • Tinned Lentils
  • Tomato Puree
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Chill, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Fry off vegetables.
  3. Add puree and veg stock.
  4. Add tinned tomatoes.
  5. Let simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve over pasta
