Taco Recipe

Description

Delicious recipe for plant-based tacos.

Umami-infused soy curls get pan-fried till crispy, tossed in a homemade taco-spiced oil, then joined by guac and salsa for (dare I say it?) the ultimate vegan tacos. One bite of these, and even skeptics will agree–these meaty, crisp-chewy soy curls taste just like chicken!

Ingredients

  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Pasta of Choice
  • Vegetable Stock
  • Tinned Lentils
  • Tomato Puree
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Chill, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Fry off vegetables.
  3. Add puree and veg stock.
  4. Add tinned tomatoes.
  5. Let simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve over pasta
